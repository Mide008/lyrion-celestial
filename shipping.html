Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the same condition you received it
  • Unworn, unwashed, and undamaged
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the receipt or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned for hygiene or custom nature reasons:

  • Earrings (for hygiene reasons)
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Final sale items (clearly marked at purchase)
  • Gift cards
  • Items purchased from third-party retailers
