Cleaning Schedule & Rituals

After Each Wear

Air & Refresh

Hang item outside wardrobe for 1-2 hours to air out. Use fabric refresh spray if needed. This extends time between washes and maintains fabric integrity.

Every 3-4 Wears

Spot Cleaning

Address small stains immediately with appropriate stain remover. For natural fabrics, try diluted white vinegar or baking soda paste first.

Seasonally

Deep Refresh

Air pieces in sunlight (briefly for colored items), steam to remove odors, check for needed repairs. Align with solstices and equinoxes for energetic reset.

Annually

Professional Care

Take special pieces (silks, heavy wools, embellished items) for professional cleaning. Schedule around your birthday for personal alignment.

"Your care routine should honor both the material and the magical. Clean with consciousness, store with intention, wear with reverence."

Essential Do's and Don'ts

‚úì DO These

  • Read care labels before first wear
  • Wash similar colors together
  • Turn garments inside out before washing
  • Use gentle, natural detergents
  • Store in breathable fabric bags
  • Repair small issues immediately
  • Rotate pieces to extend their life

‚úó DON'T Do These

  • Overload washing machine
  • Use bleach or harsh chemicals
  • Dry clean unnecessarily
  • Store in plastic (traps moisture)
  • Hang heavy knits (they stretch)
  • Expose to direct sunlight long-term
  • Ignore small repairs (they become big)

Seasonal Care Transitions

Spring Preparation (March - May)

  • Air out winter storage items
  • Check for moth damage in woolens
  • Clean before storing winter pieces
  • Repair any items needed for spring

Summer Care (June - August)

  • Protect from excessive sun exposure
  • Address sweat/salt stains immediately
  • Store delicate items away from humidity
  • Use breathable storage options

Autumn Transition (September - November)

  • Bring out and air autumn/winter pieces
  • Clean summer pieces before storage
  • Check all items for needed repairs
  • Prepare storage with fresh cedar/herbs

Winter Protection (December - February)

  • Protect from indoor heating dryness
  • Use humidifiers in closet areas
  • Rotate heavy wear items regularly
  • Check stored summer items periodically

Travel Care for Cosmic Pieces

Packing with Intention

  • Roll casual pieces to minimize wrinkles
  • Use garment bags for special items
  • Pack shoes in separate bags
  • Include a small repair kit (thread, buttons)

Hotel Care Rituals

  • Unpack immediately upon arrival
  • Hang items in bathroom during shower to steam
  • Use hotel iron on appropriate setting
  • Pack a travel-sized stain remover

Return Home Protocol

  • Air out all items before returning to wardrobe
  • Check for any travel damage
  • Clean as needed before storage
  • Thank items for their service during journey

Repair & Restoration

Basic Repair Kit Essentials

  • Thread in your most common garment colors
  • Needles of various sizes
  • Scissors (small, sharp fabric scissors)
  • Buttons (extra from your LYRƒ™ON pieces)
  • Fusible tape for quick hem repairs
  • Fabric glue for non-wear areas

When to Seek Professional Help

  • Major tears or holes in delicate fabrics
  • Complex alterations (taking in/letting out)
  • Restoration of vintage or heirloom pieces
  • Specialized cleaning for stubborn stains
  • Rebuilding structural elements (shoulder pads, etc.)

‚ö†Ô∏è Emergency Care Steps

Spills: Blot immediately, don't rub. Use cold water and mild soap. For wine, use salt. For oil, use cornstarch.

Tears: Stabilize with fusible tape immediately. Seek professional repair as soon as possible.

Odors: Air outside overnight. Use baking soda sprinkle, then brush off. For persistent odors, professional cleaning.

Sustainable Care Practices

Water Conservation

  • Wait for full loads before washing
  • Use cold water settings whenever possible
  • Choose efficient, water-saving machines
  • Reuse water for plants when appropriate

Energy Efficiency

  • Air dry whenever possible
  • Use drying racks instead of machines
  • Clean dryer lint trap after every use
  • Choose energy-efficient appliances

Product Choices

  • Select biodegradable detergents
  • Use natural alternatives (vinegar, baking soda)
  • Avoid single-use cleaning products
  • Choose concentrated products to reduce packaging
