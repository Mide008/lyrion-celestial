Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard UK delivery takes 5-7 business days. International shipping varies by location (7-14 business days). All orders are processed within 2-3 business days.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with tags attached. Custom and personalized items cannot be returned unless defective.

Do you offer custom embroidery?

Yes! We offer custom embroidery for orders of 10+ items. Contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a quote. Minimum order values apply.

How do Oracle readings work?

After purchasing a reading, you'll receive a confirmation email. Our celestial readers will prepare your personalized reading and deliver it via PDF within 48-72 hours, depending on the tier selected.
